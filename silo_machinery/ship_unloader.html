Main Filter System

Aspiration filter bag load is indicated by a differential pressure gauge.
Normally, the differential pressure should lie at about 10-15 mbar (100 - 150 mmWG). If it exceeds 20 mbar (200 mm WG), this may be due to:

  1. Filter bags dirty, to be cleaned as per maintenance instructions
  2. Controller not set properly
  3. Compressed air supply insufficient

False Air Valve

A powered false air valve allows:
- to start the blower motor while it is not yet loaded.
- immediate interruption of conveying at any time of emergency without switching off the heavy blower motor, as for instance when larger size foreighn bodies enter the suction nozzle or if changing position of the suction nozzle.
- automatic interruption of conveying in the event of blockage of the airlock and overfilling of the product separator.
